# IPM – International Property Market

IPM is a global real estate platform for agents, agencies, and investors. We provide AI-driven tools for listing management, portfolio tracking, lead and CRM workflows, and international property exposure.

## What we do
- International property listings and discovery
- AI-native workflows: listing descriptions, compliance, and insights
- Unified data core: enter once, publish to multiple channels
- CRM for buyers, sellers, and agents with personalized pipelines
- Multi-currency and ROI tools for investors
- EU data sovereignty; 2–4 week migration support
